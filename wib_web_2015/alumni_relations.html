Lillian Zuo '16
Alumni Relations Director

The Alumni Relations Chair is responsible for maintaining contact with HUWIB's alumni, as well as planning events to incorporate seniors and ensure their successful transition into alumni status. As the alumni liaison, this executive board member is the point for any alumni communication on behalf of the HUWIB undergraduates and is in close contact with the HUWIB Alumni Board Communications Chair to streamline communication and cooperation between the two boards. Partnerships between the two boards will be most centered on the Advisory Board, alumni attendance and volunteering at IBC, mentorship involvement, externship program coordination, exploratory trips opportunities, and shared networking events.
