Jane Labanowski '17
Outreach Chair

The Outreach Committee seeks to facilitate the professional development of HUWIB members by exposing them to the various opportunities available in the business world after graduation. HUWIB continues to strengthen and build international ties through the Outreach Committee. It also works to build community within HUWIB, allowing associates the chance to network intimately with domestic and international business leaders in a diverse range of industries. The Outreach Committee annually organizes Career Exploratory trips, one to New York in the spring and another to an international location in the winter. Past Career Exploratory trips have taken HUWIB Associates to Sydney, Australia and Shanghai, China.
